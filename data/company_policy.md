# Company Policy
## Leave Requests
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid time off per year. All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance.
## Remote Work
Remote work is allowed up to 3 days a week. Mondays and Thursdays are mandatory in-office days for team syncs.
## Expense Reimbursement
Travel and lunch expenses must be submitted with valid receipts within 30 days of the expense date. Maximum lunch allowance is $25 per day.

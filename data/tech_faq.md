# Technical FAQs
## VPN Issues
If you cannot connect to the VPN, first check if you have the latest version of the GlobalProtect client. If the issue persists, reboot your machine and try again, or contact the IT Helpdesk at support@company.com.
## Password Reset
To reset your password, go to the identity management portal at id.company.com. Passwords must contain at least 12 characters, including an uppercase letter, a number, and a special character.
## New Software Requests
To install new software on your work laptop, submit a ticket in Jira outlining the business justification. It generally takes 2-3 business days for approval.

# Healthy Recipes
## Avocado Toast
Ingredients: 2 slices whole wheat bread, 1 ripe avocado, pinch of salt, lemon juice, chili flakes.
Instructions: Toast the bread. Mash the avocado with salt and lemon juice. Spread over the toast and sprinkle with chili flakes.
## Quick Quinoa Salad
Ingredients: 1 cup quinoa, 2 cups water, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, feta cheese, balsamic vinaigrette.
Instructions: Boil quinoa in water until tender. Let it cool. Mix with chopped tomatoes, cucumber, and feta. Toss with vinaigrette dressing.
## Banana Oats Smoothie
Ingredients: 1 banana, half cup rolled oats, 1 cup almond milk, 1 tsp honey.
Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Serve chilled for breakfast.
